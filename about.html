RED | ROOM is a venue. It's pretty okay. We host live music every night. Except when the world shuts down. Most of our shows are good. A lot of them are great. All your favorite bands play here. Unless you have bad taste. But you don't have bad taste do you? Of course you don't.

There's also a bar. The bar's okay too. We have beer in a bottle. We also have beer in a can. There's even liquor. The liquor is in bottles too. We don't have schnapps though. And we won't make you party shots. We won't take your credit card either. You don't have to drink if you don't want to. We've got non-alcoholic options too. You should definitely buy something. It helps us keep the doors open. And it keeps the bartenders busy.

We fixed that thing you heard about so don't worry about it. The air conditioner works too. The paint is peeling off of the ceiling and we're pretty sure there's a dead rat in the wall but it's not that noticeable. What are we gonna do? Tear open the walls looking for a dead rat? It's an old building. The walls are made out of brick. Anyway, it'll decompose eventually. Don't be a jerk about it. Lighten up. Have a beer. Enjoy the show.

There's also a patio.


You can smoke out there.
